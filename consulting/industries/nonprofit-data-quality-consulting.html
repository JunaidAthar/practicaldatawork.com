Data Challenges in Nonprofit

The nonprofit industry faces a unique set of data challenges centered on donor analytics and program impact measurement. With the sector representing a $280 billion global market, the stakes for getting data right have never been higher. Organizations that invest in professional data quality consulting gain a measurable advantage in speed, accuracy, and compliance.
