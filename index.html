Age-Appropriate Programs

Infants (6 weeks - 15 months)

Gentle, responsive care with organic formula and purees. Sleep support and emotional bonding with consistent caregivers.

  • 1:4 teacher-to-child ratio
  • Organic formula and purees
  • Individual sleep schedules
  • Daily wellness reports

Toddlers (15 months - 2 years)

Mindful exploration with emotional support. Introduction to healthy foods and gentle routines that build confidence.

  • 1:5 teacher-to-child ratio
  • Cultural meal accommodations
  • Emotional regulation support
  • Outdoor play daily

Preschool (2-3 years)

Play-based learning with wellness integration. Social-emotional development through nature exploration and creative expression.

  • 1:8 teacher-to-child ratio
  • Garden-to-table nutrition
  • Mindfulness activities
  • Cultural celebration days

Pre-K (3-6 years)

School readiness with emotional intelligence focus. Academic preparation through wellness, creativity, and cultural awareness.

  • 1:10 teacher-to-child ratio
  • STEAM with wellness focus
  • Conflict resolution skills
  • Community service projects

Our Wellness Promise

Every aspect of our program is designed to support your child's physical, emotional, and cultural well-being.

Nutritional Excellence

  • 100% organic, locally-sourced meals
  • Halal and kosher options available
  • Vegetarian and vegan accommodations
  • Allergy-safe preparation protocols
  • Cultural celebration meals

Environmental Wellness

  • HEPA air filtration in all rooms
  • Natural, non-toxic cleaning products
